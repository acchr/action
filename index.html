Frequently asked questions

Does ACtion ipTV provide channels or content?

No. ACtion ipTV is a player only. It does not include, sell or stream any channels, movies or series of its own. You connect it to your own IPTV provider (an Xtream Codes account or a Stalker / MAG portal) and ACtion ipTV plays what that provider offers.

Which devices are supported?

Samsung Smart TVs running the Tizen operating system. ACtion ipTV is designed and optimized specifically for the Samsung TV remote and hardware video playback.

What do I need to get started?

A supported Samsung TV, an internet connection, and login details from your IPTV provider — either an Xtream Codes username/password and server URL, or a Stalker portal URL plus a MAC address.

How does the free trial and billing work?

You start a 7-day free trial in the app. Billing, renewals and cancellation are all handled by Samsung Checkout through your Samsung account. After the trial it renews automatically at $9.99 unless you cancel.

How do I cancel?

You can cancel anytime from the subscription settings in your Samsung account / Samsung Checkout. You keep access until the end of the current billing period.
